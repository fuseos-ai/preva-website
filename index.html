FAQ
Questions? Answers.
Is my data private?
Completely. Your AI is yours. We don't train on your conversations, we don't sell your data, and we don't share it with anyone. Your information stays in your private environment.
What can my AI actually do?
Send and manage emails, schedule meetings, search the web, create documents, manage social media, analyze files, set reminders, coordinate with your team — and it learns new skills over time. If you can describe it, your AI can probably do it.
How does it remember things?
PREVA uses a proprietary multi-layered memory system. Your AI builds a persistent understanding of you — your preferences, your relationships, your projects, your style. It doesn't just store chat logs. It actually learns.
What platforms does it work on?
Telegram, Signal, WhatsApp, Slack, Discord, email, and web chat. Your AI meets you wherever you already are — no new app to download.
Can I customize my AI's personality?
Absolutely. Your AI adapts to your communication style naturally, and you can shape its personality, tone, and behavior to match what you need — professional, casual, creative, or anything in between.
Is this just ChatGPT with extra steps?
No. ChatGPT forgets you every conversation. It can't send emails, manage your calendar, or take real action in the world. PREVA builds a persistent AI that knows you and does things for you. It's the difference between a search engine and a partner.
